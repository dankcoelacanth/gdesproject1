Be'lakor, the Dark Master

Be'lakor was a super fun model to paint up though I must admit that I did shed some blood on those wing claws of his, they're surprisingly sharp. This model was one of my first real forays into original source lighting (OSL), and it was definitely my first large model that I used OSL on. In hindsight I'd probably pull back on the lighting on the skin, but I am super proud of how it turned out on the corpse laying at Be'lakor's feet. The orange and blue contrast pretty well, even if the balance of the two is a bit out of whack.
