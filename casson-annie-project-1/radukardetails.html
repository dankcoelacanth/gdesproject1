Radukar, the Beast

The Lord Imperatant started out as an experiment before I knew what color scheme I wanted to run with for my Stormcast army. It was my first time testing out that scratchier technique on the cloak and I'm super super happy with how it turned out, that technique pops up in a good few other minis as well. It was also my first time messing around with water effects which you can kinda see on the base of the mini, in hindsight I wish I would've added some more blue to the water, it looks fine in real life but it does look a bit like shiny mud at times.
